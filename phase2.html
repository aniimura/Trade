Conclusions

  • FFD features are not useful at H1 frequency: Most FFD features ranked #32-#53 in importance
  • Session features are nearly useless: Ranked #51-#59
  • day_of_month_norm was the only valuable discovery: Ranked #3 (5.6%), but not enough to offset the noise from other new features
  • Recommendation: Keep 50-feature Pipeline-XGBoost from Phase 1. FFD may be more valuable at higher frequencies (M5, M15)
