Conclusions

  • 12.1% signal filtering: Removes low-confidence signals while preserving 83% OOS+
  • Marginal improvement: The primary XGBoost model already has high accuracy (71% profitable signals)
  • Meta model confirms market context matters: equity_momentum, corr_to_spy are top meta features
  • Recommendation: For production, Meta-0.6 provides slight improvement but adds complexity (dual ONNX). Primary-only Pipeline-XGBoost is simpler and nearly as good.

Meta-Labeling (Dual ONNX) のMT5実行

新EA (PatternDetectPhase3.mq5) が必要:
  • 2つのONNXモデルを同時ロード: Primary (50feat→方向) + Meta (52feat→フィルター)
  • Stage 1: Primary ONNX で方向予測 + 信頼度算出
  • Stage 2: Meta ONNX に 50特徴量 + primary_signal + primary_confidence を入力
  • Meta出力の take_trade_prob > 0.6 の場合のみトレード実行

改善幅が Sharpe +0.12 と小さいため、EA開発は見送り。
